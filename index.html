Band

Cardiff five piece, Basic State, combine hypnotic guitar riffs and lyrics that lust for life to create a hard hitting mix of psychedelia with inspiration from the likes of The Verve, Fontaines DC, Nirvana and The Brian Jonestown Massacre. “I premiered ‘To Feel Alive’, Lammo's (Lamacq's) a fan too. Definitely a band I want to check out live. I'm really enjoying the prog rock, psyched out vibes” - Amy Lamé (BBC Radio 6) “Sounding Excellent, I think!” - John Kennedy (Radio X)
